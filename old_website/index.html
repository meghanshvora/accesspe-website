About Us

Access Asset Managers was set up in 2011 to manage private equity investments in India. We invest in growth stage, small and mid-cap, privately held businesses.

We work mostly with first generation entrepreneurs who have the passion and commitment to strive for excellence in their businesses.

We tend to focus on businesses where we believe we can play a meaningful and impactful role in aiding the growth of the business. Hence we typically work with a small number of carefully selected portfolio companies.
