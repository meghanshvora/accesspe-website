Nasser Munjee
Advisor

Mr. Munjee brings rich and longstanding understanding of the Indian Financial Industry.

 

Mr. Munjee was the first Managing Director and CEO of Infrastructure Development Finance Company (IDFC). He was also part of the team which built HDFC, India’s first and largest mortgage finance institution. Mr. Munjee is currently the Chairman of Development Credit Bank.

 

Mr. Munjee also serves as an independent director on several corporate boards in India including Tata Motors, Tata Chemicals, Cummins India and Ambuja Cements.
