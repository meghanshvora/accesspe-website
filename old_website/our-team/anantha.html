Anantha Nageswaran
Advisor

Dr Anantha Nageswaran is an independent consultant on Investment Strategy and Macro-Economics, with over thirty years exposure in investment management.

Dr Nageswaran has served as Global Chief Investment Officer, Julius Baer and Co, until 2011. He has also worked in investment consulting at Credit Suisse, Asia Pacific.

Dr Nageswaran has published a book titled “Global Financial Markets”. He writes columns regularly for various financial papers and has several academic papers to his credit.

Dr Nageswaran holds a PhD from the University of Massachusetts (USA) and an MBA from the Institute of Management, Ahmedabad.
