Yogesh Kothari
Operating Partner

Mr Yogesh Kothari is the Chairman and Managing Director of Alkyl Amines Chemicals, a global supplier of amines and amine-based chemicals. He is also the Chairman of Diamines and Chemicals, a market leader in Ethylene Amines.

 

Mr Yogesh Kothari was on the board of DSP Merrill Lynch, a joint venture between DSP and Merrill Lynch, until eventual sale to Merrill Lynch in 2008. He has served as the Vice Chairman of the Indian Chemical Council and Member, Management Committee of Indian Merchant Chamber.

 

Mr Kothari holds a Chemical Engineering degree from University Institute of Chemical Technology (UICT), formerly UDCT, Mumbai, a Master of Science in Chemical Engineering and a Master of Management Science degree from the University of Massachusetts Lowell, USA.
