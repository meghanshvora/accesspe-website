Viren Mehta
Operating Partner

NDr. Viren Mehta brings over thirty years of experience in investment research, and strategic advisory services focused on pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries.

   

He is Founder of Mehta Partners, a Wall Street boutique investment, strategic and financial advisory firm serving global pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries since 1989.

 

Mehta Partners is also responsible for advisory work on a few significant M&A transactions in India.

 

Viren Mehta is a doctorate in Pharmacy from the University of Southern California, USA and an MBA from the University of California.
